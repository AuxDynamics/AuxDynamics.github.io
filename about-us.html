About AuxDynamics

What we do

AuxDynamics is a secure application development and testing company Founded by Rahul Sapparapu.

The organization is deeply involved in offering end-to-end IT solutions to cater to the requirements of a varied clientele worldwide. With a large team of experts, with an in-depth understanding of the innovations and resources as well as an outstanding state-of-the-art infrastructure, AuxDynamics has demonstrated its excellence in creating and designing technology-driven applications that bring more power to customers at the most affordable rates. The firm is dedicated to delivering desirable solutions within a short time frame and achieving the highest degree of customer satisfaction and loyalty. AuxDynamics sees security as basic requirement for every application, the firm empowers the developers to write secure code, by achieving rapid improvements in security compliance and consistency, as well as a better quality and speed of code writing. Along with development, Software testing, API testing, Security and penetration testing, Automated Testing, Manual Testing, Mobile Application testing and Maintenance are few other services offered by AuxDynamics.

AuxD Red Team Services Include

Penetration Testing & Ethical Hacking Tests

AuxDynamics effectively emulates sophisticated attack scenarios performed by our team of experienced ethical hackers proficient in the most advanced techniques utilized by attackers.

Vulnerability Assessment

AuxDynamics performs accurate internal and external assessments, authenticated and non-authenticated, across network devices, servers, endpoints, web applications and databases, either on premise or in a cloud environment, to help you manage your risk and improve your security posture.

Forensic Analysis

AuxDynamics digital forensics investigators use various methodologies to pursue data forensics, such as decryption, advanced system searches, reverse engineering, and other data analyses to determine the root cause and impact of an incident.

Application Security Testing and Code Review

AuxDynamics analyzes your software security from secure coding and best practices to existing vulnerabilities.

From empowering challenger organizations in building secure applications to perform security testing, we leverage technology to bring people closer to the products, content, and experiences they love.
